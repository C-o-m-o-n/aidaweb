Summary

We collect and process user data to provide and improve our AI chat service. Your data is not shared with third parties and is only accessed by authorized personnel for maintenance and abuse prevention purposes.

We collect the following types of information:

  • Discord user information (username, user ID, avatar)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the AIDA Web service

Your data is processed internally and is not shared with third parties. Only authorized personnel (specifically LyubomirT and KrokoCoder) have access to user data for the purposes of:

  • Preventing and addressing potential abuse of the system

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of your personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Request restriction of processing your personal data

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.
